Over the past several weeks of Synchronous Respect Trees and Town Halls, our community has proposed many valuable topics for discussion, and we have not yet had time to explore all of them in depth. This topic proposal suggests we could use part of our upcoming Town Hall to revisit and continue discussions on topics that received community interest but have not yet been fully addressed.

This can also serve as an opportunity to check in on any action items or follow-ups from previous discussions, see what progress has been made on ideas that were shared, and identify which topics warrant deeper exploration in future events. You can see the topics that were proposed for the most recent Eden Town Hall events in this [snapshot poll](https://snapshot.org/#/s:edenfractal.eth/proposal/0x3da213766fb092976c2d5ef29f6dd4ff76a492093263de4c6afb129abaef8bc8).
