Eden Fractal needs an Intent Document — envisioned as an operating manual that defines how the Respect Game functions, how governance processes work, and how decisions are made. Following the model established by [Optimism Fractal's Intent Document](https://optimismfractal.com/details), this document would provide a clear reference for both current and future participants. Contributions can include researching what should be in scope, drafting sections based on our current operations, reviewing how other fractal communities have structured their intents, and helping organize the document for community review and approval through our consensus process.
