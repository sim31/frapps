Eden Fractal relies on several core technical tools and welcomes contributions to their development. Priority areas include Fractalgram (the Respect Game interface), ORDAO (onchain governance infrastructure), the Eden+Fractal consensus process, and related tools or processes used by Eden Fractal. With AI-assisted development tools making it easier than ever to build applications, this is an exciting time to contribute—whether through direct improvements to our production tools or experimental applications that explore new approaches to fractal coordination.
