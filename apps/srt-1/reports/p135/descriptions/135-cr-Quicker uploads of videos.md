From what I've observed meeting recordings [uploaded to EdenCreators](https://t.me/edenfractal/5562/6262) lag by about 1 week or more.

More immediate availability of recordings would have the benefit that people who weren't able to participate could catch up immediately (say the next day).

In general, community could stay more in-sync even if not everyone is participating in meetings.

This would also be useful for people who were in the meeting and want to reference something that was discussed / said in the meeting, in the chats after the meeting.

This would also help secure state transitions of Eden Fractal. E.g.: in ORDAO it normally takes 6 days for respect distribution proposal to pass after the respect game event. A recording is one of the pieces of evidence respect-holders can use to verify respect distribution proposals to make an informed vote.

Maybe livestream would be an option and an easy way to avoid production overhead?
