During our upcoming Eden Town Hall, we can have an introduction to the Fractal Circles project from Mikael, who has been developing fractal governance concepts for over 15 years. Mikael has expressed interest in demoing his new Fractal Circles app with the Eden Fractal community and exploring ways to integrate it into our ecosystem.

Fractal Circles is a Telegram Mini App that enables collaborative proposal development and representative selection through fractal processes. During this session, Mikael can introduce himself and the Fractal Circles project, share his perspective on fractal governance, and lead us through an interactive demo where we can actually test the app together.

This provides an opportunity for the Eden Fractal community to:

* Try a new fractal governance tool in action
* Explore potential synergies between Fractal Circles and Eden Fractal processes
* Provide feedback as governance practitioners
* Connect with Mikael and the Fractal Circles project after years of working on fractal governance innovation in parallel

Fractal Circles may be a complementary tool that Eden Fractal could potentially adopt, and this demo could be valuable for any community implementing fractal decision-making processes.
