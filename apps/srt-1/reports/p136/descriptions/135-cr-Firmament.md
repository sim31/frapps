Firmament is an old project idea that has been floating around in Eden Fractal since last epoch.

My perspectives around how to achieve what I intended with Firmament, has changed dramatically, since I last talked about them in Eden Fractal.

The new project idea is much more simple and easier to deliver and could give Eden Fractal the following benefits:

⦁ Systematic way to integrate many different consensus building tools and select them from meeting to meeting
    ⦁ Would create a a space for different fractal app developers;
    ⦁ Would allow for different implementations of fractalgram (for example) to be tested;
⦁ A system to track and version governance documents effectively;
⦁ It would unite what we are currently calling legislative and executive branches into a single chain (human speed chain - 1 period - 1-2 blocks) secured by both old and new Eden Fractal Respect;
⦁ A way to market in a way that might be attractive to crypto people: concept of mining from a your laptop viable again;
⦁ A systematic way to integrate AI agents into our processes, without losing control;

I don't think that's all...

In general I think it can start the next generation of DAOs, which natively work at the level of abstraction fully understandable to all, not just smart contract developers. This development would be very aligned with where the whole tech world is going thanks to LLMs.
